Meet the Founder

A safe community for autistic & neurodivergent game developers, software engineers, and cybersecurity learners.

Photo placeholder

Doğukan Yegin

Doğukan Yegin is a game developer and cybersecurity practitioner focused on building practical, inclusive tech communities.

This society exists to help neurodivergent talent grow through real projects, mentoring, and a calm culture.

Focus

  • Game development: small projects, portfolios, game jams
  • Software engineering: clean architecture, shipping mindset
  • Cybersecurity (ethical): secure coding, CTF, blue-team basics
