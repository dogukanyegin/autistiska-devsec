Code of Conduct & Cyber Ethics

A safe culture is the product.

Respect & inclusion

No harassment, hate speech, bullying, or discrimination.

Consent & boundaries

No pressure, no doxxing, no stalking, no invasive behavior.

Legal-only security work

No illegal hacking. No malware. No targeting real systems without written permission.

Responsible disclosure

If you find a vulnerability, follow responsible disclosure practices.

Zero money rule

We do not accept donations, run fundraising, or sell services. If the project ever changes, statutes and this page will be updated.
