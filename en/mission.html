Mission & Vision

Building a society where neurodiversity is a strength in tech.

Manifesto

Neurodiversity is a natural and valuable part of human variation. We build spaces where different minds can create together — without pressure, shame, or exploitation.

What we do

  • Game & software projects: Small, achievable projects that turn into portfolios.
  • Mentorship: Peer support, code reviews, and learning paths.
  • Cybersecurity learning: CTFs, secure coding, OSINT basics — always within legal/authorized boundaries.
  • Community health: Code of Conduct, anti-harassment, and accessibility guidelines.
